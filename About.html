May 24,2019

ULTIMATE BURGER founded by Daryl burre and friends. It first open May of 2019 and located in Esperanza Street Hilltop Subdivision Q.C in front of Our Lady of Fatima University.It started with Johnnel’s love for burgers; and then one day He have this idea to make his love for burgers into a business together with his friends.At first we only served two kinds of burger the original ULTM CHEEZY BEEF BURGER and CHEEZY PORKY BURGER. After 6 months we add two more kinds of burger and added side dishes like fries and chips.

World History of burgers throughout the years hamburgers have become a symbol of American cuisine, though they are loved the world over. They come in a number of varieties, and can even be made vegetarian style. In countries like India, where beef is not part of the traditional Hindu diet, potato patties called Aloo Tikki are popular. However, the overwhelmingly favored version is the traditional beef burger. It is so popular, in fact, that the global desire for burgers may not be sustainable. Recently, scientists have been challenged to grow burger meat in petri dishes to satisfy the enormous demand for burger meat worldwide. How do you feel about meat grown in a lab?
